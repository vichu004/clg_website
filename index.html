ABOUT US

The Nehru Group of Institutions (NGI) was established in 1968 in Kuniyamuthur, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu as a center of educational excellence. NGI now comprises around 20 renowned institutes covering various fields such as Aeronautical Sciences, Engineering, Information Technology, Management, Medicine, Pharmacy, Architecture, Legal Studies, Arts.

SCHOOL OF TECHNOLOGY

Quantum Computing

Advanced quantum algorithms and computational models drive next-generation processing capabilities. Our program focuses on quantum cryptography, secure communications, and scientific research applications using cutting-edge quantum simulation techniques.

Advanced Robotics

Explore the latest advancements in robotics, including automation, machine learning integration, and real-world applications in various industries.

Augmented Reality

Create immersive mixed reality applications with advanced 3D visualization and spatial computing technologies. Master interactive design principles and user experience fundamentals for next-generation AR/VR solutions.

Artificial Intelligence

Delve into machine learning, neural networks, and AI-driven solutions that are transforming industries and enhancing decision-making processes.

Biotechnology

Explore the intersection of biology and technology, focusing on innovations in genetic engineering, bioinformatics, and medical applications.

Internet of Things

Learn about IoT architecture, sensor networks, and data analytics to create smart systems that connect devices and enhance automation.

SCHOOL OF HEALTH SCIENCE

Orthopaedics

Comprehensive musculoskeletal care covering fracture management, joint replacement, sports injuries, and postoperative rehabilitation using evidence-based protocols.

Ophthalmology

Comprehensive eye care covering cataract, glaucoma, cornea and retina services, with diagnostics such as slit‑lamp, OCT, and perimetry, and advanced medical and surgical treatments.

Infusion Pumps

Principles of volumetric and syringe pumps, flow rate calibration, alarm systems, and clinical safety practices for precise medication and fluid delivery.

Exoskeletons

Wearable robotic support systems for mobility assistance and rehabilitation, covering actuation, control, ergonomics, and clinical use cases.

MRI

Magnetic resonance imaging fundamentals including image contrast, pulse sequences, gradients, coils, safety, and clinical interpretation basics.

Dialysis Machines

Hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis systems, fluid circuits, dialyzers, ultrafiltration control, monitoring, and patient safety protocols.

SCHOOL OF SOCIAL SCIENCE

Music Performance

Vocal and instrumental performance, ear training, ensemble work, and repertoire with stagecraft and recital preparation.

Music Production & Sound Engineering

Recording, mixing, mastering, DAWs, acoustics, and live sound with studio workflows and portfolio projects.

Indian Classical Dance

Bharatanatyam/Kathak technique, abhinaya, theory, nattuvangam, and performance labs with repertoire building.

Contemporary Dance & Choreography

Contemporary techniques, composition, improvisation, kinesiology, and production design for stage and screen.

Visual Arts & Design

Drawing, painting, digital art, photography, typography, and portfolio development with exhibitions and critiques.

Theatre & Performing Arts

Acting, directing, script analysis, stagecraft, lighting and sound design with production and performance labs.
