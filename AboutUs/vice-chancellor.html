Vice Chancellor

Dr. H. N. Nagaraja

It is my great privilege to welcome you to P K DAS Institute of Social Sciences, Health Sciences and Technology (Deemed to be University), an institution built on the pillars of Academic Excellence, Innovation, and inclusive growth. In a world that is rapidly evolving, we prepare our students not only for the careers of today but for the challenges of tomorrow.

Ministry of Education on the Recommendation of UGC has issued the Letter of Intent to Nehru College of Educational and Charitable Trust to Establish " P K DAS Institute of Social Sciences, Health Sciences and Technology" Deemed to be University as Per the NEP guidelines and norms.

We are proud to offer an ecosystem where learning goes beyond books. Our curriculum is blended with real-time exposure, outbound training, research-driven projects, and a 100% placement support system that empowers students to enter the workforce with confidence and competence. At the heart of our university are our dedicated faculty mentors who guide, inspire, and walk with our students every step of the way. Supported by modern infrastructure, transport facilities, digital learning tools, and a student-friendly administration, we ensure that every learner receives a comprehensive and enriching academic experience.

Our mission is clear: to create not just graduates, but socially responsible, ethically grounded, and professionally prepared individuals who can lead in their fields and serve their communities.

I invite you to be part of this journey to discover your potential, to challenge boundaries, and to contribute meaningfully to the world.

Best Wishes
